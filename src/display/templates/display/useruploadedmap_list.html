Upload your own maps

Air Sports Live Tracking support uploading your own georeferenced map tiles for use in the navigation maps. For this purpose we support the mbtiles format that can be exported from a wide the variety of applications. If this seems intimidating, the already included OSM and OpenCycle maps provide global coverage and are in many places perfect for navigation.

For the intermediate user

MapTiles Desktop comes in both free and paid versions and supports easy georeferencing and exporting of existing maps in the mbtiles format.

For the power user

For full map control the popular QGis GIS application supports export in the mbtiles format.

Air Sport Live Tracking provides no support for the third-party applications used for generating the mbtiles files.
